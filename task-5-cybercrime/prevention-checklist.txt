1. **Verify information before sharing**
   Check the source, date, and credibility to avoid spreading misinformation.

2. **Protect personal information**
   Avoid sharing sensitive details like your address, phone number, or financial info.

3. **Use strong privacy settings**
   Regularly review and update your account privacy controls.

4. **Think before you post**
   Consider how your content might be perceived now and in the future.

5. **Recognize fake accounts and scams**
   Be cautious of suspicious profiles, links, or messages asking for personal data.

6. **Respect others online**
   Practice digital etiquette—avoid hate speech, trolling, or cyberbullying.

7. **Understand digital footprints**
   Everything you post can leave a lasting trace that may affect your reputation.

8. **Balance screen time**
   Use social media mindfully to maintain productivity and mental well-being
